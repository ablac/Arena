1. Information We Collect

We collect information in a few different ways: information you give us directly, information generated by your use of the Service, and limited technical information collected automatically. Watching the free spectator arena requires no account and no personal information from you beyond the automatically collected technical information described below. The following categories apply once you register an account or otherwise interact with authenticated parts of the Service:

  • Account information. The email address you use to sign in via magic link (or the identifier your chosen single sign-on provider shares with us), and an optional display name or chat handle you choose.
  • Chat message content. The text of messages you post in the developer chat lobby, together with the timestamp and the account that posted them.
  • Bot API keys. When you generate an API key, we store it in hashed form, not in plaintext — the same way we would store a password — so that even our own systems cannot read back your key's raw value after it is issued to you. The plaintext key is shown to you once at generation time and is your responsibility to record.
  • Bot code and configuration. The bot source code, weapon and stat loadout, and other configuration you submit to run in matches.
  • Gameplay and telemetry data. Match results, damage and movement statistics, win/loss records, leaderboard standings, connection quality metrics, and similar gameplay telemetry generated as your bot competes.
  • IP addresses. We log IP addresses for connections to the Service (including WebSocket connections from bots and spectators, and HTTP requests to our API) for rate limiting, security, and abuse-prevention purposes, as described in Section 2.
  • Payment information — handled entirely by Stripe. If you purchase a cosmetic item or subscribe to All Access, your payment card details are collected and processed directly by Stripe, our third-party payment processor. Arena never receives or stores your full card number, card verification code, or other full payment card details. We receive limited transaction metadata from Stripe — such as confirmation of a successful charge, the amount, the subscription status, and a reference token — sufficient to grant and manage the items or subscription you purchased.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information described above for the following purposes:

  • Operating the arena and matchmaking. Authenticating your account and API key, connecting your bot to live matches, running matchmaking, computing and displaying leaderboard standings, and broadcasting match state to spectators.
  • Chat moderation. Screening chat messages against blocked-keyword filters, reviewing reported messages, and applying mutes or bans as described in our Acceptable Use Policy.
  • Fraud and abuse prevention. Detecting and preventing API abuse, rate-limit circumvention, multi-accounting, payment fraud and chargebacks, and other violations of our Terms of Service or Acceptable Use Policy, including through IP address and connection-pattern analysis.
  • Service communications. Sending magic-link sign-in emails, transactional receipts, security notices (such as notice of a compromised API key), and important service announcements.
  • Product improvement. Analyzing aggregated gameplay telemetry to balance weapons and game systems, diagnose performance issues, and improve the SDK and API.

We do not use your bot code, chat messages, or gameplay data to build advertising profiles about you, and we do not run third-party advertising trackers on the Service.

3. Cookies and Local Storage

We use a small number of first-party cookies and browser local-storage entries to make the Service work, rather than for advertising:

  • Session cookie. A first-party cookie that keeps you signed in to the dashboard and developer chat after you complete a magic-link or SSO sign-in, so you do not have to re-authenticate on every page load.
  • Local storage. We use your browser's localStorage to remember display and gameplay preferences (such as graphics and animation settings) and to record that you have acknowledged this Privacy Policy or an in-product notice, so we do not show the same notice repeatedly.

We do not use third-party advertising cookies, cross-site ad tracking pixels, or third-party analytics scripts that build an advertising profile of you across other websites. Our payment processor (Stripe) and any SSO identity provider you choose to use may set their own cookies during checkout or sign-in, governed by their own privacy policies, as described in Section 5. Most browsers let you block or delete cookies and clear local storage through their settings; doing so may sign you out or reset saved preferences.

4. Your Choices

You can review and update your account email and display name from the dashboard. Where we send non-essential service communications (as opposed to essential transactional or security notices, such as a magic-link email or a compromised-key alert, which we will always send because they are necessary to operate your account), we will provide a way to opt out, such as an unsubscribe link. You can also control cookies and local storage through your browser settings as described in Section 3, and you can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information as described in Section 7.

5. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only with the following categories of recipients, and only as needed to operate the Service:

  • Stripe (payments). If you make a purchase, Stripe processes your payment directly and shares limited transaction metadata back with us, as described in Section 1. Stripe's use of your payment information is governed by Stripe's own privacy policy.
  • Transactional email provider. We use a third-party email/SMTP provider to deliver magic-link sign-in emails and other transactional messages. That provider processes your email address and message content solely to deliver those messages on our behalf.
  • Single sign-on identity provider (optional). If you choose to sign in with "Continue with SSO" rather than a magic-link email, the identity provider you select shares a basic profile identifier (such as your email address and name, depending on the provider and the permissions you grant) with us to authenticate you. Your use of that identity provider is also governed by its own privacy policy.
  • Hosting and infrastructure providers. We use third-party hosting, database, caching, and infrastructure providers to run our servers, store our database, and deliver the Service; these providers process data on our behalf under contractual confidentiality and security obligations and do not use your information for their own purposes.
  • Legal and safety disclosures. We may disclose information where required by law, subpoena, or valid legal process, or where we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of Arena, our users, or the public, or to investigate fraud, security incidents, or violations of our Terms or Acceptable Use Policy.
  • Business transfers. If Arena is involved in a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of assets, information may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to the acquiring party's commitment to honor the terms of this Policy for previously collected information, or to provide notice of any material change.

6. Data Retention

We retain account information, chat message content, bot code and configuration, and gameplay telemetry for as long as your account remains active, plus a reasonable additional window afterward to allow for account recovery, fraud investigation, dispute resolution, backup rotation, and compliance with legal obligations. Hashed API keys are retained for as long as the associated key remains valid and are deleted or invalidated when a key is revoked or rotated. Where you request deletion of your account or specific data, we will delete or anonymize it within a reasonable time where feasible, except for information we are required to retain for legal, security, or fraud-prevention purposes (for example, records needed to investigate a chargeback or a policy violation) or that persists in routine backups until those backups are rotated out in the ordinary course.

7. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information, to object to or restrict certain processing, or to receive a copy of your information in a portable format. Regardless of where you live, we offer the following to all users as a matter of practice:

  • Access and correction. You can review and update your account email, display name, and other profile information directly through the dashboard at any time.
  • Deletion and account closure. You can request closure of your account and deletion of your associated personal information by contacting us at hello@angel-serv.com. We will process such requests as described in Section 6.

To exercise any of these rights, or if you have questions about how we handle your information, contact us at hello@angel-serv.com. We may need to verify your identity (for example, by confirming control of the email address on your account) before completing a request.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service mirrors the age policy in our Terms of Service: you must be at least 13 years old to create an account, and users between 13 and 17 must have the permission of a parent or legal guardian. The Service is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 13 in the manner contemplated by COPPA or comparable laws. Watching the free spectator arena does not require an account or personal information from unauthenticated viewers.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at hello@angel-serv.com so we can investigate and, where appropriate, delete that information.

9. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards designed to protect your information, including encrypting data in transit between your browser or bot client and our servers, storing API keys and account credentials in hashed rather than plaintext form, and restricting internal access to production data to what is needed to operate the Service. Payment card data is never handled by our own systems in the first place, because checkout is processed directly by Stripe, as described in Section 1.

No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of your information. If you believe your account, API key, or credentials have been compromised, please notify us immediately at hello@angel-serv.com.

10. International Users and Data Transfers

The Service is operated from, and our infrastructure providers may process and store data in, one or more countries that may differ from your own country of residence. By using the Service, you understand that your information may be transferred to, stored in, and processed in a country other than the one in which you live, which may have data protection laws that differ from those of your home jurisdiction. Where required by applicable law, we take reasonable steps to ensure that any such transfer is subject to appropriate safeguards consistent with that law.

Your continued use of the Service after an updated Policy takes effect constitutes your acceptance of the update.
